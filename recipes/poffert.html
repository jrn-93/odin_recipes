Poffert

Description

A steamed cake with raisins and bacon

Ingredients

  • 250 g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 300 ml milk
  • 100 g raisins
  • 100 g bacon cubes
  • 1 packet yeast

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients into a batter/dough and let rise for 1 hour
  2. Pour into a greased bundt pan or mold
  3. Steam for about 1.5 hours (bain-marie method)
  4. Slice and serve with butter and syrup

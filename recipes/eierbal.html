Eierbal

Description

A deep-fried snack with egg and ragout

Ingredients

  • 4 hard-boiled eggs
  • 200 ml ragout (chicken or beef)
  • Breadcrumbs, flour, egg (for coating)
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Let the eggs cool and peel them
  2. Completely cover each egg with a layer of ragout
  3. Roll in flour, then egg, then breadcrumbs
  4. Fry at 180°C (350°F) for about 4–5 minutes until golden brown

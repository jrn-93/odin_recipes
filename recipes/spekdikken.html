Description

Savory pancakes with bacon

Ingredients

  • 250 g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 500 ml milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • Bacon cubes

Steps

  1. Make a batter with flour, eggs, milk, and salt
  2. Fry the bacon in a pan
  3. Add batter to form small thick pancakes
  4. Cook both sides until golden brown

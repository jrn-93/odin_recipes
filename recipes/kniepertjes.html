Kniepertjes

Description

Thin waffles, typically eaten around New Year

Ingredients

  • 250 g flour
  • 200 g sugar
  • 125 g butter
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients into a dough
  2. Form small balls
  3. Bake thin waffles in a waffle or kniepertjes iron until golden
  4. Serve flat (kneipertjes) or roll them into tubes (New Year rolls)

Mustard Soup

Description

A creamy soup with coarse mustard

Ingredients

  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 30 g butter
  • 30 g flour
  • 750 ml broth
  • 3 tbsp coarse Groningen mustard
  • 100 ml cream

Steps

  1. Sauté the onion in butter, add flour to make a roux
  2. Gradually add the broth while stirring until smooth
  3. Simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Stir in mustard and cream; do not boil afterward
